## Description
Walk or Pay is a commitment app that turns your daily step goal into real action — and real impact.

Set a daily step target. Deposit money. Walk for 7 days.

Every day you hit your goal, your deposit stays safe. Every day you miss, that share of your deposit goes directly to the ALS Association's Walk to Defeat ALS — funding research for people who would give anything to be able to walk.

You walk for those who can't.

**How it works:**
1. Set your daily step goal (e.g. 8,000 steps)
2. Make a deposit (e.g. €20) + €1 service fee
3. Walk every day for 7 days
4. Days you complete = money refunded. Days you miss = donated to ALS research.

Your Apple Health data is used only to track your steps. It is never shared with third parties.

Need a free pass? Each challenge includes one grace day.

**Why it works:**
Loss aversion is the most powerful motivator in behavioral psychology. When missing a day means funding something meaningful — not just losing money — the motivation is even stronger.
