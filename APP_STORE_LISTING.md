## Description
Walk or Pay turns your daily step goal into a real commitment.

Set a daily step target, deposit money, and walk. Every day you hit your goal, your money stays safe. Every day you miss it, you lose a proportional share of your deposit. At the end of the 7-day challenge, you get back what you earned.

**How it works:**
1. Set your daily step goal (e.g. 8,000 steps)
2. Deposit an amount (e.g. €20)
3. Walk every day for 7 days
4. Get back what you didn't lose — missed days cost you proportionally

Your Apple Health data is used only to track your steps. It is never shared with third parties.

Miss a day? You have one grace day per challenge to save yourself.

The money from penalties is donated to charitable causes.

**Why it works:**
Motivation fades. Money doesn't. Walk or Pay uses loss aversion — the most powerful force in behavioral psychology — to make you actually move.
